Water Crisis Awareness

There is a water crisis and it’s happening now.

2.2 billion people still do not have access to safely managed drinking water.

Safely Managed Water

Water needs to be more than clean.

Safely managed water means having water at home, whenever needed, and free from contamination.

Impact of Sanitation on Health

Without safely managed sanitation, diseases spread rapidly.

3.6 billion people – nearly half of the world – do not have access to safely managed sanitation.

Children's Vulnerability

Children are most at risk.

Every day, over 1000 children under 5 die from diseases linked to unsafe water, sanitation, and hygiene.
